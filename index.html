Another job that I am working towards is a Fighter Jet pilot in the Air Force. Currently I'm in the ROTC (Reserve Officer Training Corps) for the Air Force and I plan on joining once my career in college ends, hopefully then able to be trained as a pilot and stationed somewhere safe and useful. Most employers consider Military veterans good options for high paying jobs because they usually understand hard work and commitment as well as dedication and honesty.
